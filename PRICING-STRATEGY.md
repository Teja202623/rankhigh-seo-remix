- **$35/mo:** Priority support, advanced features

## Pricing Psychology

### Why These Price Points Work

**$19/month (BASIC):**
- Psychological: Under $20 threshold
- Competitive: Matches market leaders
- Value: ~$0.63/day = "cost of coffee"
- Annual: $228 is acceptable for small business

**$49/month (PRO):**
- 2.5x BASIC price
- Justifiable with keyword tracking + automation
- Sweet spot for growing stores
- Annual: $588 = reasonable marketing budget

**$149/month (ENTERPRISE):**
- 3x PRO price
- Agency math: Charge clients $99/mo each √ó 5 = $495/mo
- Profit margin: $346/mo makes sense
